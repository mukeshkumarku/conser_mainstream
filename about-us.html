Introductions to
Conservationmainstream

A new chapter, A new approach, A new story Conservation Mainstream is a young team that strives to link the past, tell the story of the ancient in a modern language of the present people. A door way into the past and an engagement that makes it practical in the mainstream world .

Team Members


Keerthana Muralidharan

Conservation Architect
Founder – Conservation Mainstream

Keerthana has worked with or been a part of various milestone projects like Hriday Kanchipuram temple documentation and condition assessment in Pudukkottai district, and research in Tamil Jainism and its association with the landscape. Her contribution and association with the Archeological Survey of India (ASI) is another important aspect. Her knowledge and skills in the technical part of conservation is wide and acquired through many years of personal dedication and experiences in the field.

Aishwarya KV

Conservation Architect
Co-founder – Conservation Mainstream

Aishwarya, Conservation Architect from SPA Bhopal, with a strong hold on research where she presented a paper on Agraharams of Tamil Nadu- A parametric study’ at the ‘Buildings in Society International III - An interdisciplinary Approach’ in Stockholm ’17 and ‘A strategy to conserve the Agraharams of Tamil Nadu through Community Participation’ for the Heritage management, education and practice ‘at Ahmedabad University in July 2017. She is currently working as a conservation architect in Heritage Lab at IIT Madras under Prof.Arun Menon.

Matthew Samuel

Architect and Artist

Matthew Samuel is an architect and an artist who has deep roots in heritage and culture. His passion for identifying and amalgamating historical, cultural and mythological aspects into his art is a distinct factor. He's also had experience in art and architectural writing. His works are mostly inclined towards the ancient and his skills in modern art and architecture helps him link them both. He has experience in supervising heritage conservation projects and in documentation of sites and structures of heritage value.

Vaithamanidhi L
